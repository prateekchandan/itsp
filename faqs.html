FAQS/

Frequently Asked Questions



1. What is ITSP?

ITSP is the platform where students get to the unique opportunity to unleash that hidden creativity via innovative technical summer projects. We at STAB value your creativity and thus provide complete funding of successful projects. It encompasses projects under Electronics and Robotics, Web and Coding, Astronomy, Aero-modelling and Maths and Physics.

2. Does the project need to be new/original?

New ideas are strongly encouraged. However you are free to implement a pre-existing idea.

3.Would every project be approved?

No. There will be a selection procedure for a project to be approved. You have to submit an abstract before 11:59, 10th April. The feasible projects will be selected. Hence put some thought while writing the abstract.

4. What will be there in an abstract?

The abstract should contain your idea (most important). You should provide as many details about your project as you can. Also include a tentative Plan Of Action (POA).
There will be brainstorming and review sessions with seniors to help you write your abstract. You are encouraged to take advantage of these.

5. Will I get accommodation?

Yes, accommodation will be provided in your hostel itself.

6. What is the time-frame of ITSP 2014?

There will be two slots as below. The slots will be allotted on first come first serve basis.
Slot 1:  5th May -16th June
Slot 2:  20th May - 30th June

7. I am involved in Satellite/FSAE/UMIC/URA/AUV/IGVC/ASME/BAJA project(s). Can I take an ITSP?

Yes. However a certain level of commitment is expected from your side. You must complete your work as per your plan and submit weekly reports. No flexibility regarding this will be tolerated.

8. Will there be any help sessions?

There will be sessions on microcontrollers and basic electronics. If there is any need of specific workshops, they will be conducted upon popular request. Also, mentors are allotted to every group who will guide you through your project.

9. Why ITSP? How does it benefit me?

  You choose your project and the institute funds it
  Your project will be displayed at IIT Tech-Exhibitions and exceptional projects will be displayed at TechFest as well.
  Certificate (on successful completion)
  Recognition from professors.
  Pre-internship project.
  Recommendation to Institute Tech Teams (like AUV, IGVC etc.)
  Developing contacts with seniors as well as alumni.
  Full support to innovative and commercial ideas (like patenting)

10. What is expected from me?

   Chalking out a Plan Of Action and working towards achieving your goals with full dedication
  Submission of weekly reports
  Giving presentation and demonstration for midway evaluation
  Completing the working model of your project for demonstration 1 week prior to the last day of your slot
  Submission of final project report and video by the last day of your slot
  Full support will be provided to help you achieve this in the form of mentors and sessions.

11. How do I apply?

Register your teams at Link. Create a google doc for your abstract and share it with email id 2014itsp@gmail.com as early as possible (the earlier the better for you). Your abstract will be reviewed by seniors with expertise in the relevant fields, followed by discussions (online as well as offline) and modified accordingly before finalising. Deadline: 10th April .

The skeleton of your abstract should be as follows:
1. Project title
2. Team member details (name, roll no, email-id, contact number)
3. Implementation steps (with a rough timeline)
4. Components required and their price estimate
5. What do you expect to learn by the end of the project?

12. What makes ITSPecial?

We believe freedom of thought is necessary for igniting creativity and innovation. ITSP provides you just that.

ITSP has proved to bridge the gap between an idea and actually realising it by providing a structured program and mentorship which is what makes ITSP unique.

Apart from enriching your technical skills and knowledge, you will make memories that you will always cherish. By the end of your ITSP, you will have the proud feeling of pursuing to completion that idea you always had in your mind.
