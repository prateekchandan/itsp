Call for ITSP Mentors



Why become an ITSP Mentor?

  You love doing tech and want to create a real difference in the Institute tech scene .
  As you mentor 2 teams over this summer you will get a unique opportunity to learn as you teach.
   An experience of mentorship which will stay with you for the rest of your life.
  As you mentor and co-ordinate with students, you will help them set their deadlines and a POA and make sure they achieve those.
  We will most definitely certify the help you have provided.
  Your words will influence a batch of freshmen who are the future of the IIT Bombay Tech Scene.
We plan to assign one ITSP Mentor for every 2 teams. You can be both on campus or off campus during the summer for being a part of this program. Though on campus mentors would be preferred.
